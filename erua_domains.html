ERUA

This website and the series of graphs was created by students of the masters in digital humanities at Univ. Paris 8 as part of the activities of the workshop Data visualization thaught by Prof. Everardo Reyes, in January 2023. Students cleaned and organized data that was given by ERUA staff. Then, they tested different tools and methods in order to produce the following graphs.
